Our Vision

The Climate Challenge

It is well known that the world is up for a huge task considering climate change. The UN´s sustainable development goal number 13 is dedicated solemnly to this topic because of the "catastrophic consequences" that will follow if we´re not able to limit the global carbon emissions1. The rich part of the world is responsible for around two thirds of the global climate emissions4. A wealthy country like Norway has made huge profit from these emissions, but it has been at a large cost for our planet. It is crucial to act now for the world to be able to reach the goals set at the Paris Agreement, especially the one about avoiding a temperature rise above 1.5 - 2 degrees Celcius above pre-industrial levels 5.

There are severeal consequences following climate change in quite near future. The rising temperature leads to poles melting and ocean acidification. The world also has to face more extreme weather. The UN is determined by the fact that there will be more conflicts, economic inequality, health issues and refugees around the world following climate change 4. Hunger is again on the rise, with issues following climate extremes like droughts in some parts of the world 2. Therefore, we think that it should be in every country, institution and company´s interest to engage in – and dear to invest in – solutions that strive to cope with the climate crisis in one way or another.

Engaging locally and globally

Obviously, it can be quite overwhelming for the everyday person to try and deal with all the mentioned above. One of the subgoals of our chosen UN sustainability goal is to strenghten the awareness about climate change and to make individuals and institutions able and motivated to get involved in climate actions. Our vision when making JINX, was to make it easy for people to engage both on a local and a global level. The user gets easily involved in the local area with JINX. Apart from the obvious positives following a cleaner and safer local area, we think the awareness and engagement in everyday life is just as important. At the same time, the user contributes globally by making sure that a huge amount of money finds its way straight to an ideal organization of their choice. We know there are enormous amounts of competance and engagement in the great climate-oriented organizations that exist today. But they need support, both economically and socially, to be able to get more influence and to grow.

Looking forward

With JINX, we want to transform the complex – and often quite overwhelming – topic of climate change, into something manageble and entertaining for the individuals. We strongly believe there is hope for the future. More and more voters have chosen political parties with a green profile in the recent elections in Europe particularly, especially amongst the younger population 5. That shows us that there exists a willingness to fight the climate problem we´re coping with today. We believe it is our duty to listen and invest in the future now.
