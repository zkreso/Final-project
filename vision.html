Our Vision

The Climate Challenge

It is well known that the world is up for a huge task considering climate change (1). The UN´s sustainable development goal number 13 is dedicated solemnly to this topic because there will be “catastrophic consequences” if we´re not able to limit the global carbon emissions. The rich part of the world is responsible for around two thirds of the global climate emissions (2). A wealthy country like Norway has made huge profit from these emissions, but it has been at a large cost for our planet.

Engaging locally and globally

Obviously, it can be quite overwhelming for the everyday person to try and cope with all of this. Our vision when making JINX, was to make it easy for people to engage on both a global and local level. The user contributes globally by making sure that a huge amount of money finds its way straight to an ideal organization.

Looking forward

With JINX, we want to transform the complex and – often quite overwhelming topic of climate change – into something concrete and entertaining for the individuals. We strongly believe there is hope. More and more voters have chosen political parties with a green profile in the recent elections, especially amongst the younger population (). That show us that there exists a willingness to fight the climate problem we´re living with today.
