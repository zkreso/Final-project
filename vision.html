Our Vision

The Climate Challenge

It is well known that the world has a huge task up ahead when it comes to climate change. The UN´s sustainable development goal number 13 is dedicated solemnly to this topic because there will be "catastrophic consequences" if we are not able to limit global carbon emissions1.

The rich part of the world is responsible for around two thirds of global climate emissions4. A wealthy country like Norway has profited vastly from activities that have contributed to these emissions, and which have come at a large cost to our planet.

It is crucial to act now if the world is to reach the goals from the Paris Agreement, in particular the goal of preventing a temperature rise of more than 1.5 - 2 degrees Celcius above pre-industrial levels 5, 6.

There are severeal consequences following climate change in the quite near future. The rising temperature leads to polar ice caps melting and ocean acidification. The world also has to face more extreme weather. The UN is determined to fight global warming by the fact that there will be more conflicts, economic inequality, health issues and refugees in the world caused by climate change 4. Hunger is again on the rise, with issues caused by climate extremes like droughts in some parts of the world 2.

For this reason, we believe that it should be in every country, institution and company´s interest to engage in – and to invest in – solutions that strive to cope with the climate crisis in one way or another.

Engaging locally and globally

Obviously, it can be quite overwhelming for the average person to try to deal with all of the mentioned above. One of the subgoals of our chosen UN sustainability goal is to strenghten the awareness about climate change and to make individuals and institutions able and motivated to become involved in climate actions.

Our vision when making JINX, was to make it easy for people to engage both on a local and a global level. The user becomes easily involved with their local surroundings with JINX. Apart from the obvious positives following cleaner and safer immediate surroundings, the awareness and engagement in everyday life is just as important.

At the same time, the user contributes globally by making sure that a considerable amount of money finds its way straight to an ideal organization of their choice. We know that there are enormous amounts of competence and engagement amongst all the great climate-oriented organizations that exist today. But they need support, both economically and socially, to grow and to be able to make a difference.

Looking forward

With JINX, we want to transform the complex – and often quite overwhelming – topic of climate change, into something manageble and entertaining for individuals.

We strongly believe there is hope for the future. More and more voters have chosen political parties with a green profile in the recent elections in Europe particularly, especially amongst the younger population 5. That shows us that there exists a willingness to fight the climate problem we are all faced with. We believe it is our duty to listen and invest in the future now.
